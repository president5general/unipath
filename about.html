About Unipath

Unipath was created to connect students with affordable universities worldwide, while also providing opportunities for donors to directly support students in need. Our mission is to make quality education accessible to everyone, regardless of their financial situation.

Through our platform, you can search for universities based on your field of interest, country, and budget, as well as share your personal story or contribute to helping students achieve their dreams.
